Born in Saranac Lake, NY a few miles from where the Americans triumphed over the the Russians in the 1980 Olympics, Dylan Ratigan (@Dylan Ratigan) now lives between New York City, Louisiana, California and Chicago.

Dylan is partner and co-founder of a Louisiana-based manufacturer that integrates systems to grow food, purify water, produce electricity, teach, provide wireless connectivity, create jobs and establish community security.The business was inspired by US combat veterans who described this integration of housing, food, power and water systems as central to global security.

Known by his strong voice for honest capitalist, Dylan advocates for fair markets as Chief Skeptic at Tastytrade and Advisor to CyndX.

He speaks out daily for transparency, integrity, choice and aligned interests as aspeaker, film producer, and author of New York Times Best-Seller “Greedy Bastards.”

Dylan advocates for student-debt reform, veteran employment, food and water security, drug decriminalization, renewable energy, fair markets and the recognition of money in politics as the root cause of our nations challenges. He can be found on Twitter and Facebook, or publishing on Business Insider or The Huffington Post.

Dylan is an optimist, advisor, investor, fighter for the best in all of us, and occasional surfer.
